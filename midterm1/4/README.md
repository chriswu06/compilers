# CMSC 430 --- Spring 2026 --- Midterm 1 --- Part 4


## Instructions

You've been provided an implementation of Dupe as presented in class.

Your job is to implement `odd?`, a primitive operation that determines whether a
given integer is odd.

Update *all* relevant parts of Dupe to add the `odd?` primitive to the language,
fully supporting both the interpreter and compiler.


## Notes

  - You may assume inputs to `odd?` are always integers.

  - _There are no `odd?` tests included, so you should consider writing some of
    your own.
